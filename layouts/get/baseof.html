step two

The right information, in the right place.

The world is on their phones, and your website needs to be built for mobile from the bottom up.

Getting it right on the small screens means even better results on the big ones. We call this the mock-up phase. I'll produce simple line drawings so we can understand and discuss the best way to layout your content.

mobile

tablet

desktop

You might have heard the word responsive.

What we mean is that the layout modifies itself to match the device you view it on.

All of my websites are built for the smallest screens and then big ones, which means the most important content get's placed first.

Here's the 'mock-up cycle':

  1. After fully understanding your requirements, I'll put together a whole bunch of rough layouts for you to look at.
  2. Once you've given your feedback, we'll work together over a few iterations to find something that solves your needs
  3. Now we know what content's going where, we'll start looking at color and style, what sort of feel you'd like your site to have.
  4. We'll go through these steps a few times, just to make sure were both on the same page before moving on.
